Peruvian-Style Ceviche

More Fresh Fish

If Poke isn't your thing, maybe you'll enjoy Peruvian-Style more. Citrusy and spicy.

Ingredients

  • Big ol white fish filet, like a Mahi-Mahi or Bass
  • Lemon and Lime
  • Red Onion
  • Habanero Peppers
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cube the fish
  2. Squeeze lemon and lime juice into a bowl
  3. Throw in your onion, peppers, and seasoning
  4. Marinade your fish in the bowl for 30 minutes
  5. Ready to eat!
