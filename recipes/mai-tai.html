Mai Tai

A refreshing tiki cocktail.

Enjoy this rum-forward beverage on the beach alongside your Poke.

Ingredients

  • White Rum
  • Dark Rum
  • Curacao
  • Lime Juice
  • Orgeat

Steps

  1. Add white rum, curacao, lime juice and orgeat to shaker with ice
  2. Shake, shake, shake
  3. Pour it over your glass of choice
  4. Float with some dark rum
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel, mint sprig, or anything else that sounds fun
