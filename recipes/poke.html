Ahi Poke

Fresh and Authentic

Fresh cut Ahi prepared the Hawaiian way - tossed with soy sauce, sesame oil and green onions.

No cooking needed!

Ingredients

  • Ahi
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Macadamia Nuts
  • Crushed Red Pepper

Steps

  1. Put tuna in a bowl.
  2. Put everything else in the bowl.
  3. Mix it up.
  4. Cover it up and throw it in the fridge for a couple hours.
  5. Fin.
